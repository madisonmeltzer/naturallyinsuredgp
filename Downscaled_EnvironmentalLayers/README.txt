These folders contain downscaled ascii files of environmental variables for climate scenarios RCP 4.5 and RCP 8.5 in near future (2040-2050) and far future (2070-2080).

These files are for use in Maxent as environmental variable inputs.

Environmental variables include:
- pH
- Photosynthetically Active Radiation (PAR)
- Salinity
- Current Speeds (speed)
- Sea Surface Temperature (SST)
